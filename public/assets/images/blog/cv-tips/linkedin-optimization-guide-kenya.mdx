## Overview
Kenyan recruiters rely heavily on LinkedIn. Optimize visibility, clarity, and proof of results to attract outreach.

### Key elements
1. Headline = value + function + proof  
2. About = summary with measurable results  
3. Experience = bullets with **KES**, %, or scale metrics  
4. Skills = top 15 verified  
5. Banner + photo = professional, neutral, consistent

**Example headline**
> Marketing Strategist | Grew B2B leads 3× | Digital & Brand Positioning (Kenya & East Africa)

**Tip:** Update visibility to “Open to Work (Kenya and Remote)”.
