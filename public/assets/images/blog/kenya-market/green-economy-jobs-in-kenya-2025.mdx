## Snapshot

Kenya’s shift toward clean energy and sustainable agriculture is creating new job categories. Solar, waste-to-energy, and environmental consulting are hiring at record rates.

### Hot roles
| Sector | Role | Notes |
| ------ | ---- | ----- |
| Renewable Energy | Solar project manager | 10+ large projects under REIP |
| Waste Management | Recycling operations lead | Urban expansion creates new demand |
| Agriculture | Climate-smart agronomist | NGOs funding rural sustainability |

### Required skills
Environmental policy literacy, data reporting, project financing.

**Tip:** Demonstrate quantifiable sustainability impact in CV metrics.
