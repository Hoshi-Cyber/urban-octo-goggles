## Snapshot

Kenya’s workforce will exceed **38 million** by 2030. Automation, green transition, and digital public infrastructure are rewriting role definitions.

### Shifts ahead
- 25 % of routine admin jobs will be automated.  
- Net job creation in **renewable energy**, **healthcare**, **tech services**.  
- Micro-entrepreneurship powered by mobile platforms rises.

### Key policies
Government focus: digital literacy, youth skilling, AI governance, gig worker protections.  
National Employment Authority forecasts **600 000+** digital jobs by 2030.

### What to prepare
- Upskill annually in one digital tool and one interpersonal skill.  
- Track policy incentives in energy, agriculture, and AI.  
- Build multi-income capacity: contract + side micro-venture.

Kenya’s next decade belongs to **adaptable specialists**—people who blend domain knowledge with continuous learning.
