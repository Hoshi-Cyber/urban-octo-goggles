## Overview
Kenya’s startup ecosystem attracted **USD 1.3 B** in disclosed funding by mid-2025, up 18 % YoY. Hiring rebounds strongest in fintech, logistics, and clean-energy startups.

### Top sectors
- **Fintech** — Tala, Cellulant, Turaco expanding regional teams.  
- **Logistics** — Sendy 2.0 pivots to B2B last-mile delivery.  
- **Healthtech** — Ilara Health scales tele-diagnostics.  
- **Climate & energy** — M-KOPA Energy, SunCulture adding project ops roles.

### Emerging roles
Product managers, data scientists, compliance officers, growth marketers, and customer-success leads.

### Checklist
- [ ] CV shows startup readiness (impact, adaptability, metrics)
- [ ] LinkedIn headline: “Startup | Growth | Operations”
- [ ] Portfolio links active

Startups reward proof of **execution velocity**—not years of tenure.
