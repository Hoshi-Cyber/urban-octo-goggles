## Highlights

Policy reform in competency-based curriculum continues reshaping Kenya’s education model. Edtech platforms scale regional access.

### Top shifts
- Remote learning mainstreamed for tertiary students.  
- Private academies integrate AI-tutoring.  
- Teacher demand rises in STEM and digital literacy.

**Opportunities:** Investors favour hybrid-learning and teacher upskilling startups.
