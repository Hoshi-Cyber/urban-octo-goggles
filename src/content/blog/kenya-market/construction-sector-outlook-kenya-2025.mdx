## Summary

Government housing initiatives and road infrastructure projects drive 2025 construction growth. The **Affordable Housing Program** alone adds **100K+** roles.

### Roles on demand
Civil engineers, quantity surveyors, site managers, safety officers.

**Average pay:** KES 150K–350K for mid-level professionals.

**Outlook:** Material costs stabilise, increasing private sector participation.
