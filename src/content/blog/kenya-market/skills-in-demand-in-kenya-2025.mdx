## Core takeaways

Employers prioritise **digital literacy + analytical thinking + adaptability**.  
Short-course credentials with measurable outputs beat generic certificates.

### Top 10 cross-sector skills
1. Data analytics (Excel, Power BI, Python basics)  
2. Project coordination (Agile, Trello, Notion)  
3. Sales enablement & CRM use  
4. Graphic design for marketing  
5. Compliance & ESG reporting  
6. Communication & presentation  
7. Financial modelling  
8. AI-assisted writing  
9. Leadership & collaboration  
10. Customer-success process design

**Tip:** Translate skills into proof points—“built dashboard used by 10 teams”—not lists.
