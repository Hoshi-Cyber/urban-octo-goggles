## Overview

Kenya’s tech ecosystem enters 2025 with strong fundamentals: expanded broadband, government digitisation, and global investor confidence. Startups in **AI, logistics, and payments** continue to dominate funding.

### Core metrics
- ICT contributes **10.6%** to GDP.
- Over **6 500** registered tech firms.
- Average developer salary: **KES 250 000** monthly.

### Trends
1. Fintech regulation and interoperability drive mature models.  
2. AI-enabled services (customer care, fraud prevention) scale.  
3. SaaS exports grow toward regional markets.  

### Risks
Cybersecurity breaches and currency volatility remain key risks for exporters.

**Conclusion:** Kenya’s tech scene remains Sub-Saharan Africa’s reference point for resilience and innovation.
