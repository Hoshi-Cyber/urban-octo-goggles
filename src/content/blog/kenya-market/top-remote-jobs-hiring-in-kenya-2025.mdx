## Overview

Kenya’s digital workforce is expanding fast. With reliable fibre, digital payment adoption, and time-zone alignment with Europe and the Middle East, 2025 brings more **remote roles** for skilled professionals.

### Hot functions

| Function | Typical roles | Notes |
| -------- | -------------- | ----- |
| Software & data | Front-end developer, data analyst, QA engineer | USD 1 200 – 2 500 monthly |
| Creative & marketing | Copywriter, social media strategist, motion designer | USD 800 – 1 800 monthly |
| Virtual operations | Executive assistant, project coordinator, finance associate | USD 700 – 1 500 monthly |

### Required proof
Portfolio + timezone discipline + stable internet. Recruiters value verifiable work samples more than certificates.

### Key platforms
- Deel, Remote.com, Turing, Upwork (long-term contracts)
- African talent networks like **Andela**, **Gebeya**, **RemoteBase**

### Checklist
- [ ] LinkedIn profile headline mentions “Remote | Global | Open to contracts”
- [ ] CV lists timezone and verified projects
- [ ] Payment setup tested (USD/EUR wallet)
- [ ] Internet redundancy plan in place
