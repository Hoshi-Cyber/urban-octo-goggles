## Overview

Kenya’s youth (18–35) form **75%** of the labour force. In 2025, employment expands fastest in **digital freelancing**, **logistics**, and **agri-value chains**.

### Key data
- Youth unemployment down to **9.2%**.  
- Over **1 million** freelance profiles registered on major platforms.  
- Gig work generates **KES 30B+** annually.

**Conclusion:** Employability increasingly depends on soft skills, digital literacy, and cross-sector adaptability.
