## Highlights

Universal Health Coverage rollout expands staffing across counties. Healthtech adoption accelerates diagnostics and patient management.

### Opportunities
- Nurses and clinical officers in county hospitals.  
- Health data analysts in private labs.  
- Biomedical engineers for equipment maintenance.

**Average pay:** KES 120K–280K mid-level.  
**Growth driver:** NHIF digital reforms improving coverage efficiency.
