## Overview

Farmers increasingly adopt **IoT sensors**, **mobile credit**, and **market intelligence apps**. These tools boost productivity and cut waste.

### Case studies
- DigiFarm connects 1.4M smallholders.  
- Twiga Foods expands farm-to-market logistics.  
- Apollo Agriculture scales digital lending.

**Impact:** Digital inclusion narrows urban–rural productivity gaps.
