## Snapshot

Nairobi’s property market stabilises in 2025. Mixed-use developments, affordable housing, and logistics parks lead demand.

### Metrics
- Prime office occupancy: **82%**.  
- Residential mortgage rates: **12–13%**.  
- Rental yields: **6–8%**.

**Investment focus:** logistics, data centres, and student housing.
