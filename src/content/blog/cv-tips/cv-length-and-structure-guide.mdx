## Overview
Kenyan recruiters expect brevity, order, and logical flow.

### Rules
- **Graduates:** 1 page (projects + education).  
- **Mid-career:** 2 pages (roles + outcomes).  
- **Executives:** 2–3 pages (strategy + governance + results).

### Section order
1. Summary  
2. Skills  
3. Experience  
4. Education  
5. Certifications

**Tip:** PDF format only. Fonts: Inter, Calibri, or Arial.
